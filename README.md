# LexPulse AI

## AI-Powered Vietnamese Legal Intelligence Assistant

LexPulse AI is an AI-powered legal assistant designed to help users understand, verify, and explore Vietnamese legal regulations through:

- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Knowledge Graph reasoning
- Citation-grounded AI responses
- Explainable legal analysis


---

# 1. Project Vision

Vietnamese legal documents are complex, frequently updated, and difficult for ordinary users to interpret.

Many existing AI systems can generate fluent answers but struggle with:

- Hallucinated legal references
- Incorrect article citations
- Lack of transparency
- Difficulty connecting related regulations


LexPulse AI solves this problem by ensuring:

> Every legal answer is grounded in verified legal evidence.


---

# 2. MVP Scope

LexPulse AI MVP focuses on two legal domains:


## Primary Domain

### Traffic Law

Examples:

- Traffic violations
- Administrative penalties
- Driver responsibilities


## Secondary Domain

### Labor Law

Examples:

- Employment contracts
- Employee rights
- Employer obligations


The MVP does not attempt to cover all Vietnamese legal regulations.


---

# 3. Core Features


## AI Legal Chat

Users can ask legal questions and receive:

- AI-generated explanation
- Legal references
- Evidence passages
- Confidence score


---

## Legal Claim Verification

Users can verify statements.

Example:

> "Driving without helmet results in a 500,000 VND fine."


System provides:

- Verdict
- Explanation
- Evidence


Supported verdicts:

- Correct
- Incorrect
- Misleading
- Need Context
- Unknown
- Outdated


---

## Knowledge Graph Exploration

Users can explore relationships between:

- Laws
- Articles
- Clauses
- Penalties
- Organizations
